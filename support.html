Support

CelebriDay is a daily holiday notification app that helps you discover what fun holiday it is every single day. Find the most frequently asked questions below, or reach out directly if you need more help.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I turn on notifications?

Open the app and go to the Settings tab. Tap the toggle next to "Daily Notifications" to turn them on. If prompted, allow notifications for CelebriDay. If you previously denied notifications, you may need to go to your device Settings, find CelebriDay, and enable notifications manually.

How do I change my notification time?

Custom notification times are a premium feature. Upgrade to CelebriDay Premium, then go to Settings and tap "Notification Time" to choose when you receive your daily holiday alert. Free users receive notifications at 8:00 AM.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings, scroll to the About section, and tap "Restore Purchases." Make sure you are signed in with the same App Store or Google Play account you used when you originally purchased. If your subscription is active, it will be restored automatically.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your App Store or Google Play account, not through CelebriDay directly. On iPhone, go to Settings, tap your name, then Subscriptions, and find CelebriDay. On Android, open the Play Store, tap your profile, then Payments and subscriptions. Cancel at least 24 hours before your renewal date to avoid being charged for the next period.

Why am I seeing ads?

Free users see one brief ad when they open the app. Upgrading to CelebriDay Premium removes all ads completely. Go to Settings and tap "Go Premium" to learn more.

How do I save a holiday to my favorites?

Saving favorites is a premium feature. Once you upgrade to CelebriDay Premium, tap the heart icon on any holiday card to save it. Your favorites are stored privately on your device.

Why is the notification time locked at 8:00 AM?

The custom notification time is a CelebriDay Premium feature. Free users receive their daily holiday notification at 8:00 AM. Upgrade to choose your preferred time.

The app is not showing today's holiday. What should I do?

Pull down on the Today screen to refresh. If the problem persists, try closing and reopening the app. CelebriDay includes holidays for every day of the year, so if something looks missing, a refresh usually fixes it.
