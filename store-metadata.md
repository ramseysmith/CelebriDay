## Description (App Store / Google Play)

What holiday is it today? There is always something to celebrate.

CelebriDay sends you a fun notification every morning with today's holiday, a fascinating fun fact, and everything you need to share the celebration with friends. From National Pizza Day to World Emoji Day to the quirky ones you never knew existed, there is a holiday waiting for you every single day of the year.

**DAILY HOLIDAY NOTIFICATIONS**
Wake up to a delightful notification every morning. CelebriDay tells you exactly what holiday it is, with a fun fact to go along with it. Perfect for starting the day with something to smile about.

**BEAUTIFUL HOLIDAY CARDS**
Today's holidays appear as vibrant, color coded cards on your home screen. Each card includes the holiday name, a full description, a fun fact, and a quick Share button so you can spread the joy instantly.

**FULL YEAR CALENDAR**
Browse the entire year at a glance. Every day has its own celebration, organized by category with color coded dots so you can spot your favorites from across the month. Tap any day to see all its holidays in a beautiful bottom sheet.

**365 DAYS OF FUN FACTS**
CelebriDay includes over 365 holidays with unique fun facts for every single one. You will never run out of interesting things to share at dinner.

---

**CELEBRIDAY PREMIUM**
Take your daily celebrations to the next level.

No ads. Ever. Open the app and go straight to your holidays with zero interruptions.

Save your favorites. Tap the heart on any holiday card to save it and never forget the ones you love most.

Custom notification time. Choose exactly when your daily holiday alert arrives, whether that is 7 AM with your coffee or 9 AM at your desk.

Exclusive widgets. Coming soon, Today's holiday right on your home screen.

**FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS**

How do I turn on notifications?
Go to Settings and toggle Daily Notifications on.

Can I change when I get my notification?
Yes, with CelebriDay Premium. Free users receive notifications at 8:00 AM.

How do I restore a purchase?
Go to Settings, scroll to About, and tap Restore Purchases.

---

CelebriDay is free to download. Premium features are available as a monthly ($0.99) or annual ($4.99) subscription. Subscriptions automatically renew. Cancel anytime through your App Store or Google Play account settings.
